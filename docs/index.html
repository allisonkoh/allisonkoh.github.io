Allison Koh

I am a PhD Candidate at the Berlin Graduate School for Global and Transregional Studies and the Hertie School Centre for International Security. In my dissertation research, I study the consequences of state-sponsored cyberharassment campaigns on political violence and the mobilization of dissent. I am also a Research Associate at the Hertie School Data Science Lab and the SCRIPTS Data and Methodology Center. In this role, I develop data science learning resources and consult on statistical computing for graduate students and researchers.
